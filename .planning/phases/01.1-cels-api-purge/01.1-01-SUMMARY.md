## Decisions Made
- **Observer callbacks in bridge, not tui_window.c:** The bridge file IS the flecs boundary. Keeping observer callbacks with their ecs_iter_t* signatures in the bridge avoids needing a trampoline. tui_window.c stays completely flecs-free.
- **Accessor functions over extern static:** Rather than making g_window_entity non-static or adding it to a header, added ncurses_bridge_get/set_window_entity() accessor functions. This preserves encapsulation while allowing the bridge to read/write the entity ID.
- **ncurses_terminal_init/shutdown changed to extern:** These were static in tui_window.c but need to be callable from bridge observer callbacks. Changed from static to extern linkage.
